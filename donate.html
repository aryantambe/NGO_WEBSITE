Support Us

Your donation can help provide essential skills and opportunities to differently-abled individuals across Maharashtra, enabling them to lead independent and fulfilling lives. Every contribution brings us closer to creating a more inclusive and empowered society.

Zunj Divyang Sanstha reaches out to people who are abled differently and offer support to improve their quality of life. Operating under the larger umbrella of Sevadeep, we work alongside other partner organizations to broaden its reach and impact.
